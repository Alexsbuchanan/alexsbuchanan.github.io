Current Project

Right now I am currently working on a senior thesis under Professor Matzner in the relativity group at UT Austin. The project aims to simulate the Neutron Binary system detected by LIGO (see Fun Link 2). Such a simulation involves a full general relativity simulation that uses finite differencing. It will also have to do the relativistic hydrodynamics and nuclear physics present during the merger. I am currently in charge of the portion of the code that does the general relativity. We use a set of equations called the General Harmonic Gauge with some modifications from a paper written by Frans Pretorius in 2007
